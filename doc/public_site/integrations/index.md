# Integrations

Your Trusted Planner integrates with various third-party services to enhance your workflow.

## Available Integrations

### File Storage & Sync

- **[Google Drive](./google-drive)** - Automatically sync client files to Google Shared Drives

### Payments

- **LawPay** - Accept credit card payments and manage trust accounting

### Calendar & Scheduling

- **Google Calendar** - Sync appointments with attorney calendars

### Document Processing

- **PandaDoc** - Electronic signatures and document workflows

## Setting Up Integrations

Most integrations require:

1. **API credentials** from the third-party service
2. **Configuration** in the Admin Settings
3. **Testing** to verify the connection works

Each integration guide below provides step-by-step setup instructions.
